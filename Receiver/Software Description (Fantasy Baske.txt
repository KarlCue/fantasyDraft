Software Description (Fantasy Basketball League)
 - an application that allows users to create their own fantasy league. Managers (participants) can draft their players and will be able to trade them as the season go.

Events
	- the events are trading players and drafting up to 15 players to create their own roster. 

		Peak Concurrent Events
			- Peak concurrent events could occur on trade deadlines, where managers try their best to have the best team. Trades could be up to 1000 trades per minute.

		Different Users
			Managers - participants in the fantasy league
				- usually basketball fans
			Admin - host
